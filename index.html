LATTE

Most popular coffee drink out there, the latte is made of a shot of espresso and steamed milk with a touch of foam.

CAPPUCINO

Cappuccino is a latte made with more foam than steamed milk, often with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon on top.

AMERICANO

With a similar flavor to black coffee, the americano consists of an espresso shot diluted in hot water, or sometimes ice maybe.

ESPRESSO

An espresso shot can be served solo or used as the foundation of most coffee drinks, like lattes and macchiatos.
